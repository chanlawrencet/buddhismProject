Sculpture of Buddha
Quick Facts

Found in a cave

Some year


Description

This sculpture of Buddha is standing on a small round platform, raised by a larger round black dome underneath. Made of stone, the Buddha is extremely colorful and is mostly gold color. The bigger dome underneath is made of a leaflike pattern, with rounded blobs sticking out from around the base. The color was probably originally black, but has since rubbed a bit off. The smaller pedestal on top of the dome is a goldish color.

The Buddha stands on the smaller pedestal and is extremely tall and slender. He has his arms out in front of him, with his right hand pointing up and his left hand pointing down. He is wearing a long, draped golden robe that is decorated with red rectangles. His head is a darker color but still has traces of a gold on it. On top, there is a smooth headdress that extends past his ears.
